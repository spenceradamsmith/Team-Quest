How to Play

Guess the team based on the following clues: State, Colors, Titles, Last Title, League, Conference, and Sport.

The correct team can be from any of the following:

  • NBA : Eastern / Western
  • NFL : AFC / NFC
  • NHL : Eastern / Western
  • MLB : American / National
  • NCAA Basketball : Big Ten / SEC / ACC / Big 12 / Big East
  • NCAA Football : Big Ten / SEC / ACC / Big 12

Green = Exact match.

Yellow = Close match:

  • State : Borders the correct state.
  • Colors : Only one of the colors is correct.
  • Titles : Within 3 of the correct number.
  • Last Title: Within 5 of the correct number.

Gray = Incorrect.

You get 8 guesses to find the correct team.

A new daily team is released every day at midnight (EST)!
